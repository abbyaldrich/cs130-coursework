Artists I Admire

Saorise Ronan

Saorise Ronan is an Irish actress who has starred in films such as Ladybird and Little Women. She also played Abigail Williams in the 2016 Broadway Revival of The Crucible. I admire her dedication to the craft and the diverse amount of roles she has chosen to play during her career.

Branden Jacobs Jenkins

Branden Jacobs Jenkins is an American playwright. His plays Everybody and An Octoroon are both modern adaptations of other plays. He was named a MacArthur Fellow for 2016. I admire his creativity and ability to give older plays a new, contemporary message.

To learn more about Jacobs Jenkins click here.

Joni Mitchell

Joni Mitchell is a Canadian folk singer-songwriter. Mitchell's work was most prominent in the 1960s and 1970s. The use of rich imagery in her lyrics illustrate her feelings about romance, womanhood, and social issues. I admire her lyricism and the way she addresses social issues in her music.

Donald Glover

Donald Glover is an American actor, director, producer, musician, stand-up comedian, and writer. He created and stars in the FX series, Atlanta. I am inspired by his exploration of multiple areas of art.

Remi Wolf

Remi Wolf is a contemporary American musician. Her second EP, I'm Allergic to Dogs! was released in 2020. I am inspired by the risks she takes in her art and her unique sound.

To listen to Wolf's music click here.

Laurie Metcalf

Laurie Metcalf is an American stage and screen actress. She starred in Three Tall Women and A Doll's House, Part 2 on Broadway. She starred alongside Saorise Ronan in Ladybird. I am inspired by her prolonged career on stage and screen.
