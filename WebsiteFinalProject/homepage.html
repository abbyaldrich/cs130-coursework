Abigail Aldrich

Abigail (Abby) Aldrich is a current freshman theatre major at Northwestern University from Miami, FL. She is primarily interested in acting and arts administration. She was an active participant in her high school's theatre program where she performed in many shows and directed a one-act play in her senior year. Currently, she is a Co-Marketing Director for Sit and Spin Productions and just finished up a streamed performance of Samantha Behr's The Fig and the Wasp. In her free time, she enjoys playing guitar, listening to music, and collaborating with friends on new projects.
